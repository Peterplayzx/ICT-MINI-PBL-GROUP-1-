Phishing

Phishing is a cyberattack where fraudsters impersonate trusted entities to steal sensitive data like passwords or financial details. It often happens through fake emails, messages, or websites designed to look legitimate.

Scamming

Scamming involves deceiving people to gain money, information, or assets. Scammers use tactics like fake offers, impersonation, or emotional appeals to trick their victims into handing over valuable resources.

Identity Thieft

Identity theft occurs when someone steals your personal information, such as your name, Social Security number, or credit card details, to commit fraud or other crimes. This can lead to financial losses and damage to your reputation.

Hacking

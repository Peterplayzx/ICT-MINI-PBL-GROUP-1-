Scam Awareness - Billions Lost to Scammers

Every year, scammers steal billions of dollars from unsuspecting victims. Learn about the methods they use and how you can protect yourself.

Common Scams to Watch Out For

  • Phishing: Using fake emails or messages to steal information.
  • Hacking: Unauthorized access to systems to steal data.
  • Identity Theft: Using personal data without consent.
  • Scamming: Deceiving people with false promises.

Tips to Stay Safe

  • Be skeptical of unsolicited messages or calls.
  • Do not click on suspicious links or download attachments.
  • Never share personal information over email or phone.
  • Verify identities of anyone requesting sensitive data.
  • Use two-factor authentication for account protection.
